About AAMAASA

Professional Combat Sports Training

AAMAASA is a premier martial arts and combat sports academy in North Lakhimpur, offering MMA, Kickboxing, Karate, Kun-khmer, Grappling, Taekwondo, Self-Defense, Bodybuilding, and Fitness Training for students of all ages and skill levels.

Led by Master Ahmed Ashraf, the academy combines expert instruction, discipline, and high-performance training to help students build confidence, strength, and competitive excellence.

Training Programs

Karate

Traditional martial arts focused on discipline, technique, confidence, and self-control.

Kickboxing

Kun Khmer
